Biography

I am a Ph.D candidate in Computer Science at the University of Hong Kong. During my PhD studies, I was grateful to be supervised by Prof. Wenping Wang and Prof. Taku Komura. I also had the opportunity to collaborate closely with Prof. Yuexin Ma and work under her guidance in 4DVLab at ShanghaiTech University. Prior to this, I received a B.Sc. degree from School of the Gifted Young, the University of Science and Technology of China with the speciality of Computational Mathematics.

Research interests

I am interested in applying human knowledge to robotic manipulation. This includes understanding how humans interact with their environments and adapting these skills to enhance robotic tasks.
